Frequently Asked question

1. How do I access the recipes on the search page?
To view recipes, you can use the search bar to enter keywords related to the recipe you're looking for. Once you've entered your search query, a list of relevant recipes will be displayed.

2.How can I filter the search results for specific dietary preferences or ingredients?
On the search page, you can use the filter options provided to refine your search results. Filters may include dietary preferences (e.g., vegetarian, gluten-free) or specific ingredients you want to include or exclude.

3. Where can I find the ingredients list for a recipe?
Inside the recipe popup, you'll find a section specifically dedicated to listing all the ingredients needed to prepare the dish.

4. Do you provide nutritional information for your recipes?
Yes, nutritional information is available for most recipes. You can find it listed below the ingredients in each recipe.

5.Can I view the complete recipe instructions before deciding to make it?
Absolutely! The popup menu not only displays the ingredients but also provides the complete recipe instructions. This allows you to review the steps before starting to cook.

6.How do I proceed to cook the selected recipe?
Within the recipe popup, you'll find a 'Make Recipe' button. Clicking on this button will guide you through the process of preparing the dish, providing step-by-step instructions.

7.How are recipes categorized or organized on the platform?
Recipes are often categorized based on various criteria such as cuisine type (Italian, Mexican, etc.), meal type (breakfast, lunch, dinner), dietary preferences, or cooking difficulty level. You can explore recipes by browsing these categories.
